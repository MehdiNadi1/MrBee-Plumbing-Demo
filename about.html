Who We Are

Local, Personal, and Built on Trust

Mr Bee Plumbing is a family-run plumbing and heating business based in Castle Bromwich, Birmingham. We've spent over a decade helping homeowners and local businesses across the West Midlands with reliable, professional plumbing and heating solutions.

We established ourselves as a trusted local name by keeping things simple: show up when we say we will, do the job properly, charge a fair price, and leave the place tidy. No drama, no overselling — just straightforward service that people can rely on.

As a small business, we genuinely care about every customer and every job. You won't be passed to a call centre or dealt with by someone who doesn't know what they're doing. When you call Mr Bee Plumbing, you get a professional who will listen, advise honestly, and get the job done right.
